Disease Library

Rose Plant Conditions

Our AI can detect these 5 conditions from a leaf photo

✅

Healthy Leaf

No Disease

The leaf shows no signs of infection, damage, or stress. Vibrant green color, smooth surface.

Tip: Water at the base, not on leaves. Check weekly.
🍄

Black Spot

Fungal

Circular black or dark brown spots on leaves. Caused by the fungus Diplocarpon rosae.

Fix: Fungicide spray + remove infected leaves.
💧

Downy Mildew

Water Mold

Yellow patches on top of leaves, greyish mold underneath. Spreads in cool humid weather.

Fix: Copper fungicide + improve airflow.
🦠

Rose Rust

Fungal

Orange or rust-coloured powdery pustules on the underside of leaves. Spreads fast in warm weather.

Fix: Sulfur-based fungicide spray weekly.
🐛

Rose Sawfly

Insect

Tiny larvae eat through leaf tissue leaving see-through or skeletonized patches on leaves.

Fix: Neem oil spray + manually remove larvae.
